## Design Context

### Users
Quantitative researchers, strategy builders, and discretionary traders reviewing U.S. equity market regimes week by week. They need a compact workspace for checking regime labels, feature drivers, and strategy implications without exposing API credentials in the browser.

### Brand Personality
Calm, analytical, decisive. The product should feel like a research desk tool: dense enough for repeated use, visually crisp enough to trust, and restrained enough to keep attention on market state rather than decoration.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light institutional dashboard with ink-like typography, sharp data blocks, regime colors, and charts as the main visual assets. Avoid marketing-page composition, generic dark trading-terminal glow, and oversized hero copy.

### Design Principles
- Show the latest regime and the full five-year context immediately.
- Make every label auditable through the underlying metrics and rule notes.
- Keep controls compact and table-first for research workflows.
- Use regime color consistently across heatmap, timeline, table, and detail views.
- Treat data freshness and methodology as first-class UI elements.
